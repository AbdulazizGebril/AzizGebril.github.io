Top Refugee Origins

According to UNHCR, Humanitarian crisis and poltical uurests caused the migration and forced displacement of more than 26 Million refugee by the year 2020. The bubble chart below shows the top refugee origins. More than 60% of refugees under the UNHCR's mandate originate from five countries. These countries are Syria, Venezuela, Afghanistan, South Sudan and Myanmar. unfortunately, these countirs went through major poltical unrests or harsh economic crises.


Top Refugee Destinations

Around 86% of the refugees under the UNHCR's mandate are hosted in developing countries. In addition, 39% of refugees are hosted by five countries, These countries are Turkey, Colombia, Uganda, Pakistan and Germany. One observation is the close distance of proximty of those five countries with the Top refugee origins countries.
